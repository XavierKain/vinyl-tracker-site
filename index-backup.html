FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Vinyl Tracker work without internet?

Yes! Vinyl Tracker is built offline-first. Your entire collection is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and localStorage. You can browse, search, and edit your catalog without any internet connection. You only need internet when scanning a new barcode to fetch album data from MusicBrainz.

Can I import my Discogs collection?

Absolutely. You can export your Discogs collection as a CSV file and import it directly into Vinyl Tracker. The app will match your records against MusicBrainz to fill in any missing metadata. You can also import from any standard CSV format.

What devices does it run on?

Vinyl Tracker is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it runs in any modern browser on any device — iPhone, Android, iPad, laptop, desktop. You can install it to your home screen for a native app experience. No app store needed.

Is my data safe? Where is it stored?

Your data lives entirely on your device. We don't have servers storing your collection — it's 100% local. You can export your entire catalog as JSON or CSV at any time for backup. Your data is yours; we never see it, sell it, or lose it.
